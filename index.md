> **About This Platform:** The Iowa Action Desk provides direct, data-backed advocacy routing to reduce friction for civic engagement. We analyze municipal budgets and state legislation through a lens of economic solidarity, ensuring working-class communities retain a clear voice in public policy. This repository is maintained entirely by an independent private citizen operating in a personal capacity to support democratic self-determination.

## Active Action Alerts

*   **Municipal Budget Comment Window:** The local city manager budget proposal review process has opened. Review the local sandbox directory for public hearing dates and coordinated neighborhood scripts to resist the extraction of community resources.
*   **Legislative Status:** The Iowa General Assembly is currently adjourned. Statewide legislative alerts remain online for historical review and tracking, but current updates are focused primarily on municipal and county budget windows.
