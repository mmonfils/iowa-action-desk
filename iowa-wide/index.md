## Find Your Iowa Legislators

Before using the action scripts below, you must identify your specific house and senate district representatives. State legislators prioritize communications from verified constituents living within their geographic boundaries.

*   [Find Your Legislator via Iowa General Assembly Portal](https://www.legis.iowa.gov/legislators/find)

Once you enter your home address in the portal above, write down your state representative and state senator names to use in the following action scripts.

---

## Guide: How to Look Up Committee Assignments

Many bills are held up in specific committees before they ever reach the house or senate floor for a general vote. To target the right politicians, use these steps:

1. Click the main legislation search tool on the state portal.
2. Enter the specific House File or Senate File number.
3. Look at the current history line to see which committee currently holds the bill.
4. Call the legislative switchboard and ask to speak directly with the chairperson of that specific committee to request a sub-committee hearing.
