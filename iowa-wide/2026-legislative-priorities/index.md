## Active Behavioral Health Alerts

### Psychiatric Hospital Bed Expansion
Advocate for increasing adult and youth bed counts at state psychiatric hospitals to address regional care gaps.

Require structured, safe discharge plans for individuals transitioning out of psychiatric hospitalizations.
*   <a href="{{ site.baseurl }}/iowa-wide/2026-legislative-priorities/responsible-discharge-planning/" style="display: block; padding: 10px; margin: 5px 0; background-color: #159957; color: white; text-align: center; border-radius: 0.3rem; text-decoration: none;">Action Script: Discharge Planning</a>

Increase non-emergency structured subacute care locations to establish stable community step-down options.
*   <a href="{{ site.baseurl }}/iowa-wide/2026-legislative-priorities/subacute-care-expansion/" style="display: block; padding: 10px; margin: 5px 0; background-color: #159957; color: white; text-align: center; border-radius: 0.3rem; text-decoration: none;">Action Script: Subacute Care Options</a>

Add dedicated long-term housing infrastructure with integrated support services for residents facing chronic illness.
