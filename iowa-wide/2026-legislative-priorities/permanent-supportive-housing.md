# Action Alert: Permanent Supportive Housing

### 1. Policy Briefing
*   **Situation:** Residents managing severe, long-term mental illness face systemic exclusion from local housing markets, triggering high rates of chronic homelessness.
*   **Background:** Corporate housing acquisitions and rising market rates have removed affordable inventory across Iowa metro areas. Regional tracking via the [Iowa Finance Authority](https://www.iowafinance.com) notes a wide deficit in housing units tied directly to clinical care teams.
*   **Assessment:** A person-in-environment perspective shows that clinical wellness is impossible without basic housing security. Forcing sick individuals to sleep on streets represents an extraction of human dignity that breaks neighborhood networks.
*   **Recommendation:** Establish a dedicated state housing development line to build permanent supportive housing models for our most vulnerable residents.

### 3. Sample Script
Connect the request to your personal observations of community stress before stating the policy demand.

> "Hello, my name is a constituent living in your area. I am calling because I see how our community feels the strain when public resources are extracted from our neighborhoods. We see our most vulnerable neighbors left out on the streets without shelter while dealing with profound chronic illness. We need a system that actively invests in neighborhood stability rather than creating structural friction for regular families. I urge you to allocate state funding for permanent supportive housing options. Will you commit to voting in favor of this public measure?"
