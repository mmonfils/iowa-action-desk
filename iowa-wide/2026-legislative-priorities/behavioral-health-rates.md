# Action Alert: Medicaid Provider Rates

### 1. Policy Briefing
*   **Situation:** Low Medicaid reimbursement rates force community mental health centers to cut vital regional care programs or completely pull out of Iowa.
*   **Background:** Public reimbursement rates paid out to clinicians have failed to track real overhead and labor costs, creating massive wait times across rural and urban centers alike.
*   **Assessment:** This structural underfunding extracts wealth from public health safety nets. When provider rates stay flat, working-class communities lose their care networks, forcing vulnerable residents to go entirely without baseline clinical support.
*   **Recommendation:** Mandate an immediate, permanent increase to public healthcare reimbursement rates across all community service settings.

### 3. Sample Script
Connect the request to your personal observations of community stress before stating the policy demand.

> "Hello, my name is a constituent living in your area. I am calling because I see how our community feels the strain when public resources are extracted from our neighborhoods. Regular working families in our town have to wait for months just to get a basic counseling appointment because clinics cannot afford to keep staff. We need a system that actively invests in neighborhood stability rather than creating structural friction for regular families. I urge you to vote for an immediate increase to our baseline provider reimbursement rates. Will you commit to voting in favor of this public measure?"
