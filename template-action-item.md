# Action Alert: Standard Topic Name

### 1. Policy Briefing
*   **Situation:** Describe the immediate problem occurring in the community right now.
*   **Background:** Provide historical context detailing how the system arrived at this point. Include credible primary source hyperlinks to ground facts and figures.
*   **Assessment:** Analyze how this problem creates systemic friction for working-class residents using a person-in-environment perspective and systems theory. Include direct in-text links to verifiable public metrics or source dashboards.
*   **Recommendation:** Propose the clear structural solution grounded in economic solidarity.

### 3. Sample Script
This script connects localized public data with an explicit, values-centered request.

> "Hello, my name is a constituent living in your area. I am calling because I see how our community feels the strain when public resources are extracted from our neighborhoods. I urge you to support the policy proposal to fix this issue. We need a system that actively invests in neighborhood stability rather than creating structural friction for regular families. Will you commit to voting in favor of this public measure?"
