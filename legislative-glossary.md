# Grassroots Policy Glossary

This plain-language guide defines common municipal and state budget terms to build technical literacy across our local neighborhoods.

### General Fund Levy
The core property tax rate used by cities and counties to pay for daily operations, including libraries, parks, and local safety workers.

### Statutory Cap
A firm limit placed into state law that stops local governments from raising their revenue levels beyond a fixed percentage, regardless of local needs.

### Extraction Loop
A system where community wealth or public funds are funneled away from local neighborhoods and paid out to out-of-state companies or private contractors.

### Medicaid Reimbursement Rate
The fixed dollar amount that public insurance programs pay out to doctors and counselors for delivering medical care to low-income residents.

### Subacute Care
A level of medical care for individuals who do not need a full emergency hospital bed but still require professional support before returning home.

### Local Self-Determination
The democratic principle that local neighborhoods and town governments should have the legal power to control their own budgets and public policies.
